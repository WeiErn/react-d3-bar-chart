1. In command prompt,

a. enter 'npm install'
b. after download is complete, enter 'npm start'

2. In browser,

a. after entering 'npm start', you would be redirected to localhost:3000 in your browser, if not go to localhost:3000 by keying it into your browser's address bar

b. The bar chart of documents is displayed on the landing page. Click on one of the three options 'Confidentiality', 'Documents', 'Languages' to view its corresponding chart

c. However over the bars in the chart to reveal the total number of documents for each sub-category of the selected main categories
